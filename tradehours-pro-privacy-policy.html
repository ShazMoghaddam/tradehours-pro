TradeHours Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2026  ·  Developer: Shaz Moghaddam

🕐 Short version: TradeHours Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your preferences are saved locally on your device only. The app works fully offline after the first load.

1. Who We Are

TradeHours Pro is developed and published by Shaz Moghaddam ("we", "us", "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the address listed at the end of this document.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. TradeHours Pro does not require an account, login, or any personal details to function.

The following data is stored only on your device using localStorage and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your selected dark/light theme preference
  • Your selected grid or list layout preference
  • The list of markets you have added to your personal dashboard
  • The display order of your market cards (drag-to-reorder state)
  • Any ambient light or display preferences you have configured

3. Network Requests

TradeHours Pro is designed to work offline. The only outbound network requests made by the app are:

  • Google Fonts — on first load, the app fetches Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. These are then cached locally. This request exposes your IP address to Google's servers. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Holiday API (optional) — if you configure a live holiday data API endpoint, the app will call that URL. This feature is disabled by default. We do not operate this API and have no control over any third-party provider you choose to connect.

We do not operate any back-end servers. We do not receive any data from your device.

4. Permissions

TradeHours Pro may request the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required to load Google Fonts and optional holiday data.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to detect whether the device is online or offline, so the app can show the appropriate status.

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device resource.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We use no analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking of any kind. We have zero visibility into your usage of the app.

6. Device Sensors

TradeHours Pro uses the ambient light sensor (if available on your device) solely to adjust screen brightness for a better viewing experience. Sensor readings are processed entirely on-device and are never recorded or transmitted.

7. Children's Privacy

TradeHours Pro is not directed at children under 13 years of age and is intended for use by traders and investors. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Data Security

All app data is stored in your device's localStorage, which is protected by your operating system's security model. We do not maintain any servers that hold user data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
